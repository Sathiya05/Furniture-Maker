The 12-Week Commission Journey

01

Weeks 1-2: Curation & Design

We begin with a wood-species consultation and architectural sketching. During this phase, we hand-select the raw timber flitches from our sustainable partners to match your desired grain pattern.

02

Weeks 3-8: The Master Build

The timber is brought into the studio for milling and traditional joinery. This is where we execute hand-cut dovetails and friction-fit tenons. You will receive weekly photo updates of the progress.

03

Week 12: Studio White-Glove Delivery

The final piece is inspected, signed by the artisan, and delivered via our white-glove service to ensure perfect placement and on-site assembly within your space.
