The Finishing Details

Perfection in the
Master's Touch.

A piece isn't complete until it speaks to the senses. We forgo synthetic lacquers, opting instead for hand-rubbed natural oils and waxes. This pulls the deepest chatoyancy from the wood grain, creating a buttery-soft tactile experience that wears its history with pride.

Hand-Rubbed Oils

Organic linseed and beeswax blends that nourish the timber from the inside out.

Premium Accents

Integrating unlaquered brass, blackened steel, and full-grain leather hardware.
