Our Manifesto

The Discipline of Hand & Machine

We believe in a disciplined balance. While modern precision ensures the structural perfection required for architectural stability, only the human hand can interpret the unique grain narrative of a specific walnut flitch.

Our philosophy is rooted in the quiet hours of hand-planing and the relentless pursuit of joinery that lasts for generations. We do not just build furniture; we engineer heritage pieces that breathe and age gracefully alongside your family.

Studio Standard

Guaranteed Structural Integrity

The Method

From Forest to Floor

01. Selection

Hand-curating sustainable timber flitches based on grain density and moisture stability.

02. Dimensioning

Precision milling and rough-cutting to specifications while respecting the wood's tension.

03. Joinery

Executing traditional friction-fit dovetails and tenons for a lifetime of structural integrity.

04. Finishing

Applying multiple layers of hand-rubbed organic oils to enhance the natural lustre.

The Final Polish

Refinement Without Compromise

Every piece undergoes a multi-stage refinement process. Before any oil is applied, we conduct a structural integrity check and grain-flow inspection to ensure the piece meets our 25-year mastery standard.

6-Stage Hand Sanding

Starting from rough-milled to ultra-fine 400 grit for a velvet-touch surface.

Zero-VOC Organic Oils

Sustainably sourced, plant-based finishes that protect the wood and environment.

Lifetime Structural Check

Testing tension and joinery tolerances to guarantee stability across seasonal changes.
